Kurt Vonnegut

29 January 2009

Kurt Vonnegut is a feisty original. Ever since reading Breakfast of Champions there are certain thick black sketches that stick in my mind. His books are short but shoot straight to the point.

The Sirens of Titan, Cat’s Cradle

Science-fiction dolled up in its literary finest.

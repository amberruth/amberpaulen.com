Good Books Listed by Author /

Alice Munro

5 October 2011

Canada’s queen of the short story. Actually, the English speaking world’s queen of the short story.

The Beggar Maid

An early compilation of short stories, or is it a novel? Following Rose’s life, and occasionally Flo’s, the stories work nearly chronologically, but always leave the reader putting together the bigger pieces of these two characters’ lives.

The Progress of Love

A collection of short stories mostly coinciding around certain poignant moments, changes along the course of love. My introduction to Alice Munro, and well, I’m hooked.

Good Books Listed by Author /

Angela Carter

6 January 2012

Angela Carter seems to be enjoying a posthumous resurgence of popularity and rightfully so. Her works are impressive and fly off the scale of imagination and intelligence.

The Infernal Desire Machines of Doctor Hoffman

Perhaps by the title it’s evident that this book will take you someplace you’ve never been. And that assumption is correct. There is so much layered into this book that it will pay off, even more, on a second reading. Fantastic, heroic and strange.

Nights at the Circus

What a book! Set in London, St. Petersburg, and Siberia, the story follows Fevvers, a woman with wings, as she builds her own story and embarks on becoming the first free and flying woman.

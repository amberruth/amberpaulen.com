Fyodor Dostoevsky

27 January 2009

Would works of words exist if it weren’t for the illustrious Fyodor Dostoevsky? I reckon not. Back to this giant of a writer I dare say we could trace any book that’s worth reading. For if you write and have not awed, than what good are you?

Crime and Punishment

Of course.

The Brothers Karamazov

It’s very difficult to declare one book as The Best Book, but if I must than this would be it.

The Idiot, The Adolescent, The House of the Dead, Demons

Why not read everything with his name on it? They all rival each other’s genius. This is a funny story as told by General Ivolgin from The Idiot.

Short Stories: The Eternal Husband, The Double, The Dream of a Ridiculous Man, The Gambler

Dorothy Allison

21 June 2011

My fascination with Dorothy Allison’s works has begun, and will continue when I can get my hands on more of her books. Seeing her tell a short story and answer questions at John Cabot University in Rome, perhaps has something to do with it, but there’s no denying the power of her voice.

Bastard Out of Carolina

This book shot Dorothy Allison into the known literary world, and with good reason. Not a light story, but damn good.

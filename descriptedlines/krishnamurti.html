Krishnamurti

28 January 2009

I digress, Krishnamurti was not a writer. A man with a message, like a prophet, he walked the earth talking. He spoke to those who would listen, to those who would take his words to heart. The message he was determined to relate is oh! so relevant today. There are many compilations of his talks, among them:

Think on These Things, The First and Last Freedom, The Awakening of Intelligence

Krishnamurti: The Years of Awakening

Biography by Mary Lutyens. To understand the words of Krishnamurti it was vital that I understood some bit of his life. The Years of Awakening describes Krishnamurti’s childhood into adulthood, until the dissolving of The Order of the Star.
